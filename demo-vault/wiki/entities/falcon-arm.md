# Falcon Arm

The Falcon Arm is a product line of [[Acme Robotics]]. Orbital Dynamics recently signed a supply agreement to provide precision actuators for the Falcon Arm, marking the first direct collaboration between the two companies since Jane Rivera left Orbital Dynamics to found Acme Robotics.
