# Acme Robotics

Acme Robotics is a robotics firm based in Seattle, Washington. It was founded by Jane Rivera after leaving Orbital Dynamics. Acme Robotics has recently entered into a supply agreement with [[Orbital Dynamics]] to provide precision actuators for the Falcon Arm product line.
