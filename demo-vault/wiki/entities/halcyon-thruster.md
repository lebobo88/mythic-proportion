# Halcyon Thruster

The Halcyon Thruster is a flagship product line of [[Orbital Dynamics]]. It is a satellite propulsion system managed by lead engineer [Dana Kwan], who joined Orbital Dynamics in 2020 after working alongside [Marcus Webb] at a prior aerospace startup.
