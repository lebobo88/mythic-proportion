# Acme Robotics Project Notes

Acme Robotics is a robotics company based in [Austin], [Texas]. Acme Robotics was founded by [Jane Rivera] in [2019]. [Jane Rivera] serves as the CEO of Acme Robotics and previously worked as a senior engineer at Orbital Dynamics.

Acme Robotics is currently developing a product called the Falcon Arm, an industrial robotic arm designed for warehouse automation. The Falcon Arm project is led by [Tom Chen], who reports directly to [Jane Rivera].

Acme Robotics has a partnership with Meridian Logistics, a logistics company that will deploy the Falcon Arm across its distribution centers. Meridian Logistics is headquartered in [Denver], [Colorado], and is run by CEO [Priya
Anand].

[Tom Chen] previously collaborated with [Priya Anand] on an earlier automation pilot at Meridian Logistics before joining Acme Robotics full-time.
