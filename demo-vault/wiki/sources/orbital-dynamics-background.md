# Orbital Dynamics Background

Orbital Dynamics is an aerospace engineering firm based in Seattle, Washington. Orbital Dynamics was co-founded by Marcus Webb and Elena Farr in 2015. Marcus Webb currently serves as Chief Technology Officer, while Elena
Farr serves as Chief Executive Officer.

Orbital Dynamics builds satellite propulsion systems, including the flagship Halcyon Thruster product line. The Halcyon Thruster program is managed by lead engineer Dana Kwan, who joined Orbital Dynamics in 2020 after working alongside Marcus Webb at a prior aerospace startup.

Orbital Dynamics recently signed a supply agreement with [[Acme Robotics]] to provide precision actuators for the Falcon Arm product line, marking the first direct collaboration between the two companies since Jane Rivera left Orbital Dynamics to found Acme Robotics.
